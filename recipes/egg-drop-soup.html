Egg Drop Soup

This recipe for Egg Drop Soup is from The Woks of Life.

Egg drop soup is an easy to make and comforting soup. It has few ingredients, and can be made in 15 minutes or less.

Ingredients:

  • 4 C chicken stock
  • 1/2 tsp. sesame oil
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp sugar
  • 1/8 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric (optional for color)
  • 3 Tbsp. cornstarch (mixed with 1/3 C water)
  • 3 eggs (lightly beaten)
  • 1 scallion (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp MSG (or to taste)

Steps:

  1. Bring stock to a simmer in medium pot. Stir in sesame oil, salt, sugar, white pepper, and MSG. Add in turmeric, if using. Taste the soup, and add seasoning if needed.
  2. Add the cornstarch slurry to the soup slowly, stirring continuously. You can add more or less cornstarch if you'd like a thicker or thinner consistency.
  3. Add the egg while stirring the soup. The speed at which you stir will determine the size of the egg swirls in your soup. Stir slower for bigger swirls.
  4. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with scallions. Serve!
