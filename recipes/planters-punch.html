Planter's Punch


This is a recipe from liquor.com

A Planter's Punch is a rum-based tropical drink that is a bit on the sweeter side, thanks to the grenadine.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. dark rum
  • 1 oz. simple syrup
  • 3/4 oz. lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 tsp. grenadine
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 splash club soda, chilled
  • Garnish: mint sprig or cherry and orange

Steps:

  1. Add dark rum, simple syrup, lime juice, grenadine, and bitters into shaker with ice. Shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into Collins glass over crushed ice, and top with a splash of club soda
  3. Garnish and serve!
