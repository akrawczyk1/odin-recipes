Lasagna


This is a Lasagna recipe from The Cozy Cook

It uses ricotta cheese for creaminess, and it includes a hearty meat sauce and a layer of melted mozzarella on top. It also uses no-boil lasagna noodles to make the cooking process a little easier.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese Filling
    • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 C mozzarella cheese
    • 3/4 C Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
    • 2 tsp. Italian seasoning
    • 1/2 tsp. salt
    • 1/4 tsp. pepper

  • Meat Sauce
    • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
    • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
    • 3/4 lb. ground beef
    • 3/4 lb. Italian sausage
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/2 C chicken broth
    • 40 oz. marinara sauce
    • 1 Tbsp. tomato paste
    • 1 tsp. hot sauce
    • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

  • Lasagna Noodles / Cheese Topping
    • 12 lasagna noodles, plus extra in case of breakage
    • 2.5 C mozzarella cheese, grated

Steps:

  • Prep Work:
    1. Combine cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out remaining ingredients.

  • Make the Meat Sauce:
    1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add diced onions. Let soften for 10 minutes.
    2. Add ground beef and sausage; increase heat to medium high. Use spatula to break up meat so it's very fine and crumbled. Cook for 8-10 min, or until cooked through. Add garlic during last minute. Drain.
    3. Add chicken broth, use a spatula to "clean" bottom and sides of pot. Add marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Add fresh herbs if desired, such as parsley and basil. Bring to a boil, then simmer. Cover partially and let sauce simmer gently.
    4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F

  • Assemble:
    1. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce on bottom of 9 x 13-inch casserole dish. Then, add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping if necessary.
    2. Spread 1/3 of ricotta cheese mixture over noodles, followed by ~1 1/2 C meat sauce.
    3. Top with 4 more lasagna noodles, and repeat previous step. Do this step again so you have 3 total layers of noodles, cheese, and meat sauce.
    4. Top with 2.5 C Mozzarella cheese.
    5. Spray aluminum foil with cooking spray and place spray-side down on baking dish.
  • Bake:
    1. Bake for 35 minutes covered. Remove cover and bake for additional 10 minutes.
    2. Garnish with fresh parsley and basil.
    3. Let sit for 10-15 minutes to allow layers to set. Cut and serve!
