Lasagna


This is a Lasagna recipe from The Cozy Cook

It uses ricotta cheese for creaminess, and it includes a hearty meat sauce and a layer of melted mozzarella on top. It also uses no-boil lasagna noodles to make the cooking process a little easier.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese Filling
    • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 C mozzarella cheese
    • 3/4 C Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
    • 2 tsp. Italian seasoning
    • 1/2 tsp. salt
    • 1/4 tsp. pepper

  • Meat Sauce
    • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
    • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
    • 3/4 lb. ground beef
    • 3/4 lb. Italian sausage
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/2 C chicken broth
    • 40 oz. marinara sauce
    • 1 Tbsp. tomato paste
    • 1 tsp. hot sauce
    • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

  • Lasagna Noodles / Cheese Topping
    • 12 lasagna noodles, plus extra in case of breakage
    • 2.5 C mozzarella cheese, grated
